For urgent issues please call 911 or contact clinic for immediate response. Response for messages would be given within 24-48 hours.

As your medical home, it's important that we make it easy for you to communicate with us. We are happy to take your appointment request via a portal message. If you message us during business hours, you can expect a reply within 10 minutes. If it is after-hours, a reply will be sent tomorrow. Business hours are: Monday through Friday 7:00 AM-8:00 PM and weekends 8:00 AM-5:00 PM.

As your medical home, we welcome the opportunity to assist you in registering for programs that make healthcare more accessible and affordable. Please remember to bring the following items to your program registration interview: proof of address (such as a utility bill), a picture ID, and proof of all income coming into the household within the last 30 days. If you have additional questions about what documents are needed, please call 512-686-0534

As your medical home, the hospital welcomes input regarding member concerns and grievances. Portal messages are delivered to our Patient Experience Team. You may also address your safety and quality of care concerns to:
• Any Clinic Manager
• Patient Communication Coordinator: 1-855-572-2896 (toll-free)
• Director of Quality: 512.686.0207
• Human Resources: 512.686.0207
• HHSC Civil Rights Office (for discrimination): HHSCivilRightsOffice@hhsc.state.tx.us

Welcome to the Health Information Management (“Medical Records”) department. To request a copy of your medical records, do not send a portal message. Instead, please complete the form entitled Authorization to Release, Use, or Disclose Information. The signed form should then be faxed to 512-506-8085 or scanned and emailed to MedicalRecords@test.org. All forms will be processed within 72 hours.

For all billing questions, please include your Account Number and the Date of Service in the message. If you would prefer to speak to a representative in person, you may call the Billing Department at 512-931-2190.
